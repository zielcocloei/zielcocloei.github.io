About NASA

NASA stands for National Aeronautics and Space Administration. Established in 1958, NASA is a U.S. government agency responsible for science and technology related to air and space. Its mission is to explore the universe, advance aeronautics research, and push the boundaries of innovation for the benefit of humanity.
